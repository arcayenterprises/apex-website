Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Can my child do more than one program? +
Yes. Programs are designed to work independently but players can participate in multiple. A common combination for competitive players is Summer Camp in July alongside the Advanced Training Program, then Preseason Prep in August.
How does the 50% multi-camp discount work? +
Your first camp week is $297. Every additional week you register for is $148.50. There is no limit on how many weeks you can book at the discounted rate. You can register for all four camp weeks if you choose.
Who is the Advanced Training Program for? +
This program is for players born 2016 and older who are currently training at a competitive or advanced level. It is not suitable for recreational or beginner players. All applications are reviewed by our coaching staff before placement is confirmed.
What is the Preseason Prep program and who should apply? +
This is our most intensive summer offering, designed specifically to prepare competitive players for the fall season. It combines soccer training led by our Technical Director with athletic development through our YAD partner. Players must be born 2016 or older and training at an advanced or competitive level. Spots are very limited and assessed individually.
Do existing Apex members get priority? +
Yes. Current Apex members have first access to all summer programs. Remaining spots are opened to external registrations after the member priority window closes. If you are a current member and have not received your priority registration information, contact us at admin@apexfootballacademy.com.
Is there a refund policy? +
Please contact us directly at admin@apexfootballacademy.com for questions about cancellations or transfers.
